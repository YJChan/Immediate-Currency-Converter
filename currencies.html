GUIDE
How to use this extension
  1. Select your base currency and currency you want to convert.
  2. Select(Highlight) any number on your web page, then right click for context menu.
  3. Select Convert From X to X from context menu. a converted value will display to you in notification.
For more feature
  1. Click on "show side bar" or open side bar with "Ctrl + Shift + Y (Windows or Linux) / Cmd + Shift + Y (Mac)"
  2. Select Immediate Currency Converter from sidebar.
  3. You can copy and view your conversion history and convert 1 currency to all available currency!
